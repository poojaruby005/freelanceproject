ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Incorporated in the year 2019, Deepji Pumps Pvt. Ltd. is a supplying company of pump sets of more than fifty different varieties of submersible pump, self-prime pumps, and centrifugal pump sets customized, especially according to the cli-ent’s needs.

We market these pumps under the brand name “Turaco” and sell through our wide-spread and extensive distribution network across South India. We have our registered corporate office in Bengaluru (Karnataka).
