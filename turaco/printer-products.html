ABOUT TURACO SELF PRIME MONO BLOCK PUMPS

Turaco Self Priming Pumps are designed for household application and trouble-free operation. Best quality material and components are used to give a noise-free, vibration-free running for a long time.

It is very important to choose the right model as per requirement. This product catalog with complete pump details will help you to choose the correct model as per your requirement.

Installation of the Pumps by a proper qualified Plumber is very important to ensure its performance.
