About us

It is a fact that, as there is a long term huge demand for security and related field, ABA has made its core strategy to exhibit values by performance. We are fully aware about the sensibility and sensitivity involved in this profession. Our responsibility is to secure highly professional, specialized, expertized as per expectation of our valued customers.

We create work force having awareness to manage any kind of risk and provide entire business protection. Being a very sustainable ethical business is a key to the ongoing success of ABA.

Our overall behavior, culture and performances are the signatures of our organization which mark ABA standard.

In simple words, we are committed to quality service, we can say our entire hard working team striving hard to achieve this and we keep on put our efforts to do what we promised.

Features:

  • Our service is 24x7 round the clock.
  • Managed by ex-servicemen, senior officers and experienced executives.
  • We provide an accommodation for the candidates (those who are from outside Bangalore) and we provide grocery to them for their day to day need.
  • Our personnel are thoroughly verified, certified and stringent documentations are done before recruitment.
  • Day and night patrolling to keep the staff on alert.
  • Our personnel are between the age group of 20-35 years.
  • Educated and physically fit with a good and neat appearance in dress code.

We are here in order to give a justified customer’s value added service. Therefore, if you have any queries or issues, further you are always welcome to call us/reach us and feel free for any clarifications and information’s.
